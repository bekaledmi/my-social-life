All your data — people directory and logs — lives in your Google Sheet. The app loads from it on every open.

1
Go to sheets.new → name it My Social Life
2
Extensions → Apps Script → paste the apps-script.js file
3
Deploy → New deployment → Web app → Execute as: Me → Anyone → copy URL
4
Paste the deployment URL below → Connect. The app will push your 100 people and load everything from Sheets from then on.
